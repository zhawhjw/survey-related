Poses in same color group are chosen by same method. Each group contains 5 poses. These poses are supposed to be similar to the BASE pose. We call them similar poses

Please choose only one of groups that has most of similar poses. Notice that "similar" does not necessary to be identical with BASE, but be close enough in your opinion. Also, the order in same color group (top to down or 1st to 5th) is crucial (i.e. 1st one should be similar to BASE one than 5th one does).

Hence, you can choose group based on two category 1) counting numbers of similar poses in same color group 2) judging order of these similar poses makes sense. Chose the one you think is best.
